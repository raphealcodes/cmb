trading information

  • All times are in GMT+2

  • Instant Execution (Fill or Kill) is applicable only for instruments with fixed spreads. We guarantee the price, but not execution.
    That means, if the price has changed, your order won't be executed at a different price and you will get a requote.

  • Market Execution (Click & Deal) is applicable for all instruments that have a floating spread. This type guarantees you immediate
    execution of your order but the price could change during the process of opening your position. If the price has changed, you
    won't get any requotes and your order will be filled immediately at the new price.

  • The basic Leverage is 1:100. However, upon further request and choosing a different account type, we offer leverages up to 1:500.
    For additional information see our Account Types page or contact your account manager.

  • Swaps are charged at the end of each trading day at 24:00 GMT+2; A 3-days swap is charged on Wednesday at 24:00 GMT+2

  • The swap value is according to the symbol's base currency â€“ and is per day per lot at 24:00 GMT+2.

  • Exotic currency pairs trading could be limited during the trading sessions in case of extremely low volatility or a high one.

  • If you have opened a long stock position prior to the ex-dividend date, you will receive 80% of the dividend's net amount.

  • If you have opened a short stock position prior to the ex-dividend date, 100% of the dividend's net amount will be debited from
    your balance.

  • When trading Futures, please note that if you have any open positions by the expiration date, they will be automatically closed
    at the last received (closing) price.

  • Commission for every asset will vary and change, depending on the account type. Commissions will be anything from 0 up to
    -100 EUR/GBP/USD per 1 lot invested depending on the account and terms and conditions for every client and account by itself.

  • A Stop-Out has been set at 0% (percent) for all clients of Grandefex in order to prevent a full loss occurring on an account if or
    when a negative (losing trade/s) are occurring or have already occurred.

  • After a full restriction on an account has been imposed, the Grandefex team will close all open trades/positions on that account
    until full verification procedure has been completed.

  • Due to high or low market volatility and security purposes our risk management can change the trading conditions individually
    without any notification in advance.

  • The value of your investments can go down as well as up. Losses can exceed deposits on margin products. Complex products,
    including all types of investment assets and instruments, come with a high risk of losing money rapidly due to leverage. You
    should consider whether you understand how all types of investment assets or any of our other products work and whether you
    can afford to take the high risk of losing your money or making money.
